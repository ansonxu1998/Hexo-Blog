矩阵的导数运算(1)

矩阵的导数运算 (1)

标量方程对向量的导数：

定义： \[ \frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial \vec{y}}=\begin{bmatrix}\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_1}\\\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_2}\\\vdots\\\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_m}\end{bmatrix}_{m\times 1} \]

  • \(f(\vec{y})\) - 标量 [\(1\times1\)]
  • \(\vec{y}=\begin{bmatrix}y_1\\y_2\vdots\\y_m\end{bmatrix}_{m\times1}\) - 向量 [\(m\times1\)]

求导结果的行数与分母相同，称为分母布局(Denominator Layout)

\[ \frac{\partial f{\vec{y}}}{\partial\vec{y}}=\begin{bmatrix}\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_1}&\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_2}&\dots&\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_m}\end{bmatrix}_{1\times m} \]

行数和分子相同，称为分子布局(Numerator Layout)

例

\(f(y_1, y_2) = y_1^2+y_2^2\)，\(\vec{y}=\begin{bmatrix}y_1\\y_2\end{bmatrix}\)

\[\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial \vec{y}}=\begin{bmatrix}\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_1}\\\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_2}\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}2y_1\\2y_2\end{bmatrix}\] 分母布局

\[\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial \vec{y}}=\begin{bmatrix}\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_1}&\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_2}\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}2y_1&2y_2\end{bmatrix}\] 分子布局

向量方程对向量的导数

\[\vec{f}(\vec{y})=\begin{bmatrix}f_1(\vec{y})\\f_2(\vec{y})\\\vdots\\f_n(\vec{y})\end{bmatrix}_{n\times1}\] \[\vec{y}=\begin{bmatrix}y_1\\y_2\\\vdots\\y_m\end{bmatrix}_{m\times1}\] \[ \frac{\partial\vec{f}(\vec{y})_{n\times1}}{\partial\vec{y}_{m\times1}}=\begin{bmatrix}\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_1}\\\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_2}\\\vdots\\\frac{\partial f(\vec{y})}{\partial y_m}\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}\frac{\partial f_1(\vec{y})}{\partial y_1}&\frac{\partial f_2(\vec{y})}{\partial y_1}&\dots&\frac{\partial f_n(\vec{y})}{\partial y_1} \\ \vdots&\ddots&&\vdots\\ \frac{\partial f_1(\vec{y})}{\partial y_m}&\frac{\partial f_2(\vec{y})}{\partial y_m}&\dots&\frac{\partial f_n(\vec{y})}{\partial y_m}\end{bmatrix} \] 分母布局

常用特例

若 \(\vec{y}=\begin{bmatrix}y_1\\\vdots\\y_m\end{bmatrix}_{m\times1}\), \(A=\begin{bmatrix}a_{11}&\cdots&a_{1m}\\\vdots&\ddots&\vdots\\a_{m1}&\cdots&a_{mm}\end{bmatrix}_{m\times m}\), 则 \(\frac{\partial A\vec{y}}{\partial\vec{y}}=A^T\)

若 \(\vec{y}=\begin{bmatrix}y_1\\\vdots\\y_m\end{bmatrix}_{m\times1}\), \(A=\begin{bmatrix}a_{11}&\cdots&a_{1m}\\\vdots&\ddots&\vdots\\a_{m1}&\cdots&a_{mm}\end{bmatrix}_{m\times m}\) , 则 \(\frac{\partial\vec{y}^TA\vec{y}}{\partial\vec{y}}=A\vec{y}+A^T\vec{y}\). 当 A 为对称矩阵时，\(A^T=A\), \(\frac{\partial\vec{y}^TA\vec{y}}{\partial\vec{y}}=2A\vec{y}\)

\(\vec{y}^TA\vec{y}\) : 二次型
